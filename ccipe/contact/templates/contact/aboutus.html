About Us

China Center of International Politics and Economy was established in 2008 as a China-based independent think tank. CCIPE aims to support the decision making process with evidence-based research. CCIPE contributes to the formulation of innovative strategic options and to the better understanding of International Economics and Finance, International Security, Big Powers Relation, Diplomatic and Security policy research, International Crisis Management, Energy and Climate Change, and Non-traditional Security. CCIPE contributes to debates and discussions at the civil society level and maintain a smooth communication with policy makers.

The main purpose of CCIPE is to strengthen international communication on domestic and foreign policy issues, to enhance participation in the policy development process and to promote an issue based problem solving approach. CCIPE aims to contribute to the better understanding of the priorities and insights of global politic and economic issues.
